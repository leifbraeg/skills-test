# Tone Guidelines

## Formal
- Use complete sentences
- Avoid contractions
- Technical terminology
- Third person when possible

## Casual
- Conversational language
- Contractions allowed
- First/second person
- Relatable examples

## Sarcastic
- Ironic statements
- Witty observations
- Double meanings
- Use of emoji for tone

## Encouraging
- Positive language
- Affirmations
- Supportive tone
- Action-oriented phrases
